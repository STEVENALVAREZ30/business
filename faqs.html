Frequently Asked Questions

What types of water do you offer?

We offer purified drinking water and mineral water, both delivered fresh to your doorstep.

How can I place an order?

You can place an order directly on our website or by calling our customer service.

What is the delivery charge?

Delivery charges vary depending on your location. You can check delivery charges at checkout.

Is there a minimum order?

Yes, the minimum order is 1 gallon of water for delivery services.

Do you offer subscription plans?

Yes, we offer flexible subscription plans for regular deliveries. Contact us for more details.
